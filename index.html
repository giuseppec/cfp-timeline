ML Conference Deadlines and Timeline

This is a fork of the original CFP timeline site (source on GitHub), with a narrower, ML/AI-focused conference set, improved filtering options, an adjustable timeline window, and updated styling. It aggregates conferences from the CORE rankings and the GII-GRIN-SCIE (GGS) ratings, adds Google Scholar's H5 metrics, cross-references them with their calls for papers on WikiCFP for key dates, and places them on a timeline. Missing dates may be inferred from previous conference editions.

If you see missing or incorrect data, please update or create the corresponding WikiCFP entry. You can also report wrong CFP matches or other issues on GitHub.

About the filters

H5Index2024 / H5Median2024

These sliders let you filter conferences by recent citation impact. H5-index and H5-median are citation metrics reported by Google Scholar for venues. Higher values mean that more of the venue’s recent papers are cited often. The values were scraped from the public Google Scholar interface, so some conferences may have missing or incorrect values.
Source: https://scholar.google.com/intl/de/scholar/metrics.html#metrics

CORE/GGS Rankings

Filter by conference quality tiers (A++, A+, A, …). These tiers come from international ranking initiatives such as CORE and GGS, which assess venue prestige and selectivity.
Sources: CORE https://portal.core.edu.au/conf-ranks/
GGS https://scie.lcc.uma.es/gii-grin-scie-rating/conferenceRating.jsf
